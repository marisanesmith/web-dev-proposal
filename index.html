Objective

The objective is to overhaul the existing 'Brand Name' to better highlight it's unique features and drive more customers and clients to the site. The focus of your new site will be:

  • visually stunning design
  • simple, user-friendly navigation that encourages users to browse further
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimized) site for strong rankings for relevant key words
  • fast to load and compatible across all modern browsers
  • easy for non-technical users to update and maintain
  • mobile and tablet friendly (responsive) design
  • built in the latest web technologies (HTML, CSS, Javascript & Jekyll)

Website Process

Step 1 -Strategy: We engaged in a strategic meeting with relevant key parties to discuss goals, the brand, important features and functionality, etc. We will then come back with a comprehensive website strategy including design inspiration.

Step 2 - Design: Based on the strategic brief and in line with the style of the 'Brand' and target audience, we will come up with a custom design for the website. This design will be optimized not just to be visually stunning but to load fast, be easy to update & maintain and planned with an understanding of the user. This stage will take around 2 weeks.

Step 3 - Development: After you have reviewed and approved the design we will get to work building it. We will be building from scratch using HTML, CSS, Javascript & Jekyll. We will conduct comprehensive browser testing, speed testing and website optimization including setting up with Google Analytics. This stage will take around 3 weeks.

Step 4 - Review & Upload Content: Now it's time to review the live site (on a hidden domain), upload all of your content (photos, logos, etc) and teach you how to use the CMS (Content Management System) so it's easy for you to manage your website.

Step 5 - Site Live: When all has been signed off, we will push the site live on the client name domain and conduct one final round of testing.
